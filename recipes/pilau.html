Pilau

Description

A simple recipe for preparing pilau, a dish associated with the coastal peoples of East Africa

Ingredients

  • Rice: Kenyan pishori rice, Basmati rice could be used in some cases
  • Beef: 1/4 grass fed beef steak
  • Pilau masala: An indigenous spice that gives the dish its famous pilau flavour
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Red Onions
  • Water
  • Olive oil: Vegetable oil could be substituted for this

Steps

  1. Prepare the rice then keep it for later use
  2. Fry the diced beef in olive oil, add diced red onions, ground ginger and garlic
  3. Cook for about two minutes then add pilau masala to give it an intrinsic pilau flavour
  4. Add water and simmer till the beef is soft and te water is more than three quarters draned
  5. Add the rice then cook for about three minutes
  6. Serve hot and enjoy
